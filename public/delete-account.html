1 Account Deletion Overview

MulhimIQ allows users to initiate account deletion directly from within the mobile application. Both student and teacher accounts support account deletion.

When you submit a deletion request, your information is recorded and your account is scheduled for permanent deletion within 30 days, subject to legal and regulatory retention requirements.

2 How to Delete Your Account

  1. Sign in to the MulhimIQ app.
  2. Open your Profile page (Student: My Profile · Teacher: Profile in the teacher dashboard).
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Submit your deletion request on this page with your account details.
  5. Your request is reviewed and your account is deleted within 30 days.

3 What Happens After You Submit

  • Your deletion request is recorded securely.
  • Your account and personal data are scheduled for deletion within 30 days.
  • After submitting a deletion request, account deletion enters the review and processing stage. Personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days, subject to legal and regulatory retention requirements.
  • Your profile will be removed from active use once deletion is completed.
  • Educational or financial records required by law may be retained for a limited period.
  • Remaining personal data is deleted or anonymized according to our privacy policy.

4 Data Retention Policy

Some records may be retained when required by legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, educational, or regulatory obligations.

After the required retention period, retained data is deleted or anonymized in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

5 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I continue using the app after submitting a request?

A: Account deletion requests are processed according to MulhimIQ's account deletion policy. Personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Q: When will my account be deleted?

A: Within 30 days of submitting your deletion request.

Q: Can I restore a deleted account?

A: Once deletion is completed, accounts may not be recoverable.

Q: Does this apply to teachers and students?

A: Yes. Both account types are supported.

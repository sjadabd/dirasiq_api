Delete Your Account – MulhimIQ

You can delete your account directly from within the MulhimIQ app.

How to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the MulhimIQ mobile application.
  2. Go to your Student Profile screen.
  3. Tap on "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once confirmed, your account will be removed from active use. This delete process performs a soft delete which prevents access to your profile and associated data.
